Chorba Recipe

Chorba is the number one soup in Turkmenistan.

This dish is enjoyed as a first dish in the menu and served at homes, grand events and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 100ml oil of choice
  • 400gr of beef
  • 200gr of carrots
  • 2 heads of onion
  • 3 heads of medium size potatos
  • 2 heads of large tomatoes
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Cut meat into chuncks and set aside.
  2. Peel the carrots, cut into bite size cubes, wash and set aside
  3. Peel the potatos, cut into bite size cubes, wash and set aside
  4. Wash, cut the tomatoes into bite size and set aside
  5. Peel onions, cut into small pieces and set aside.
  6. Get a non-stick pot, heat it up on the stove and add the oil.
  7. Toss the meat in and fry on high heat until light dark color.
  8. Toss in the onions and fry until solf.
  9. Toss in the tomatoes and simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  10. Add 500ml of water and bring to boil. Add a table spoon of salt.
  11. Lower the heat and add carrots and tomatoes. Bring to boil.
  12. Once the water boils reduce the heat. Cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes.
  13. Check the carrots and potatos. If it is cooked, turn off the heat and steam for 1-2 minutes.
  14. Taste. Should be cooked and seasoned by now.
  15. Serve hot.
  16. Enjoy!
